Vous \u00eates d\u00e9veloppeur\u2022euse, data scientist, designer, et vous avez envie de vous engager pour transformer le service public\u00a0? Rejoignez la deuxi\u00e8me promotion Entrepreneur\u2022e d\u2019Int\u00e9r\u00eat G\u00e9n\u00e9ral\u00a0!<\/strong><\/p>\n

Navigation maritime, donn\u00e9es de sant\u00e9, mobilier national, accompagnement des entreprises, donn\u00e9es de la recherche, archives, innovation sociale : venez mettre vos talents et expertises au service de d\u00e9fis d\u2019int\u00e9r\u00eat g\u00e9n\u00e9ral\u00a0!<\/p>\n

Initi\u00e9 en janvier 2017 par la Pr\u00e9sidence de la R\u00e9publique, le Programme Entrepreneur\u2022e d\u2019Int\u00e9r\u00eat G\u00e9n\u00e9ral<\/a> consiste \u00e0 recruter pour dix mois des innovateur\u2022trice\u2022s ext\u00e9rieur\u2022e\u2022s \u00e0 l\u2019administration, afin de relever des d\u00e9fis li\u00e9s \u00e0 l\u2019utilisation des donn\u00e9es dans le secteur public et \u00e0 la transformation num\u00e9rique de l’\u00c9tat.<\/p>\n

Un nouvel appel \u00e0 candidature est aujourd’hui lanc\u00e9. En janvier 2018,\u00a027 EIG formeront la Promotion 2.<\/p>\n

Acc\u00e9der \u00e0 l’appel \u00e0 candidature, ouvert jusqu’au 27 octobre 2017 :\u00a0EIG – Promo 2<\/a>
\n<\/strong><\/p>\n<\/blockquote>\n

Le programme est port\u00e9 par Etalab au sein du SGMAP, en lien avec le Commissariat g\u00e9n\u00e9ral \u00e0 l\u2019investissement et la Caisse des D\u00e9p\u00f4ts et Consignations.<\/em><\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Des d\u00e9fis \u00ab\u00a0tous terrains\u00a0\u00bb \u00e0 r\u00e9soudre en \u00e9quipe<\/span>
\n<\/em><\/span><\/h2>\n

Les EIG de la Promotion 2 auront 12 d\u00e9fis \u00e0 r\u00e9soudre. Ils ont \u00e9t\u00e9 s\u00e9lectionn\u00e9s par un jury d\u2019experts dans le cadre d\u2019un appel \u00e0 projets organis\u00e9 de mai \u00e0 juillet 2017. A ces 12 d\u00e9fis s\u2019ajoute un d\u00e9fi de coaching technique de la Promotion 2, pr\u00e9vu dans l’appel \u00e0 projets.<\/p>\n

Plusieurs nouveaut\u00e9s\u00a0: <\/strong><\/p>\n

Diversit\u00e9 –<\/span><\/span>\u00a0<\/strong> Les d\u00e9fis<\/a> recouvrent des domaines vari\u00e9s n\u00e9cessitant des comp\u00e9tences rares : projets de data science, d\u00e9veloppement de plateformes num\u00e9riques et d\u2019interfaces, design de services, gestion de projet, etc.<\/p>\n

Compl\u00e9mentarit\u00e9<\/span> – <\/strong>Chaque d\u00e9fi est dimensionn\u00e9 pour accueillir 2 \u00e0 3 entrepreneur\u2022e\u2022s aux savoir-faire compl\u00e9mentaires. Sur les 27 EIG \u00e0 recruter : <\/strong>9 d\u00e9veloppeur\u2022euse\u2022s, 5 designers, 1 expert\u2022e en gestion de projet num\u00e9rique, 2 g\u00e9omaticien\u2022ne\u2022s et 10 data scientists (dont 1 expert\u2022e en datavisualisation et 1 data manager).<\/p>\n

Engagement<\/span> – <\/strong>Afin de r\u00e9aliser ces d\u00e9fis ambitieux, les administrations laur\u00e9ates se sont engag\u00e9es \u00e0 constituer, autour des EIG, des \u00e9quipes projets, regroupant diff\u00e9rents m\u00e9tiers (SI, expertise li\u00e9e \u00e0 la nature du d\u00e9fi, etc.). Ces \u00e9quipes seront pleinement impliqu\u00e9es dans la conception, le d\u00e9veloppement, et la r\u00e9alisation des d\u00e9fis. Cette configuration vient en renfort du dispositif de mentorat, mis en place d\u00e8s la Promotion 1. Chaque EIG est accompagn\u00e9\u2022e d\u2019un mentor op\u00e9rationnel<\/strong> et suivi\u2022e par un mentor strat\u00e9gique<\/strong>, positionn\u00e9 \u00e0 haut niveau dans l’administration afin de faciliter l\u2019int\u00e9gration du travail de l\u2019entrepreneur\u2022e. La promotion se r\u00e9unira toutes les 2 semaines pour des sessions collectives de partage d\u2019exp\u00e9rience et de suivi de projet.<\/p>\n

Il est possible de postuler \u00e0 plusieurs d\u00e9fis.<\/p>\n

          Developers, data scientists, designers: come transform French public administration.\u00a0 Join the second cohort of the EIG program! <\/strong><\/p>\n

          If you are interested in sea navigation, health data, culture, government support for companies, research data,\u00a0 archive management or social innovation, apply now to improve public administration with your talents and skills.<\/p>\n

          The Entrepreneur\u00b7e d’Int\u00e9r\u00eat G\u00e9n\u00e9ral <\/em>program (or EIG<\/em> for short) was initiated by the French Presidency in autumn 2016 and launched in January 2017. The key principle: recruiting innovators from outside government to tackle 10-month-long challenges linked to the use of public data and the digital transformation of government.<\/p>\n

          A call for applications was launched on September 27th 2017. In January 2018, 27 Public Interest Entrepreneurs will form the Season 2 of the Program.<\/p>\n

          The call for applications is open until October 27th, 2017: EIG – Season #2 (in French)<\/a>
          \n<\/strong><\/p><\/blockquote>\n

          The program is run by Etalab (a mission of SGMAP, the Secretariat-General for Government Modernisation), in conjunction with the Commissariat g\u00e9n\u00e9ral \u00e0 l’investissement and the Caisse des D\u00e9p\u00f4ts et Consignations.<\/em><\/p>\n

          Diversity, teamwork, commitment<\/span>

The agencies will host the Public Interest Entrepreneurs for 10 months to work on the following challenges:<\/p>\n

            • Gobelins\u00a0<\/strong>: unveil the riches of Mobilier National’s furniture collections by digitally opening them to the public<\/a>, Ministry of Culture, Mobilier National<\/em><\/span><\/li>\n
            • Pr\u00e9visecours\u00a0<\/strong>: help fire brigades intervene more effectively with predictive models, <\/a>Ministry of the Interior<\/span><\/li>\n

              The main features of this season 2:
              \n<\/strong><\/p>\n

              Diversity<\/span> –\u00a0<\/strong> The challenges<\/a> encompass a wide range of areas requiring high-level skills: data science projects, development of digital platforms and interfaces, service design, project management, etc.<\/p>\n

              Teamwork<\/span> – <\/strong>Each challenge will be solved by 2 to 3 Entrepreneurs with complementary skills. The 27-person team will be made up of: 9 developers, 5 designers, 1 expert in digital project management, 2 geomaticians and 10 data scientists (including 1 datavisualisation expert and 1 data manager).<\/p>\n

              Commitment<\/span> – <\/strong>To carry out these ambitious challenges, each government agency will assemble a project team around the Public Interest Entrepreneurs, bringing together specific skills needed to solve the challenge. These teams will be fully involved in every step of the challenges: design, development, and implementation. They will also complement the mentoring system set up during Season #1. Each Entrepreneur is accompanied by an operational mentor and benefits from the overview of a strategic mentor whose senior position in the hierarchy facilitates the smooth implementation of the EIG’s work. The cohort will meet every two weeks for group sessions to share their experiences and discuss their progress.<\/p>\n

              Find out more about the challenges and the types of missions in the call for applications<\/a> (in French)<\/em><\/strong><\/p><\/blockquote>\n

It is possible to apply for several challenges.<\/p>\n

                • \u00ab\u00a0Technical\u00a0\u00bb skills: tech (programming languages and frameworks, fullstack development), UX design, data science.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/blockquote>\n
